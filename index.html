This site is where I write down what I'm building, noticing, and trying to understand.

A lot of what interests me sits at the intersection of AI agents, developer tooling, enterprise workflows, and the systems that help people actually get work done. I tend to come at these topics as a builder first: curious about architecture, grounded in real constraints, and more interested in durable usefulness than hype.

I do not write because I think everything needs to be marketed. In fact, probably the opposite. I usually write when something feels real enough, strange enough, or important enough that it is worth trying to explain clearly.

So this is a place for working ideas.
